# Conto — Development Journal

> **How to use this file:** Add an entry every time you sit down to work. Note your start/stop time, what you did, and tag it with a category. At the end of each sprint, fill in the sprint summary. This file lives in the root of your repo.

> **Category tags:** `[learn]` `[backend]` `[android]` `[devops]` `[debug]` `[design]` `[docs]`
